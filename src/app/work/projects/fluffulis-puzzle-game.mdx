## Overview

**Fluffulis** is a meditative 3D puzzle game created to help players **decompress while challenging their minds**. Set in a peaceful dreamscape, players guide a soft cat-like creature through spatial puzzles, with the goal of reaching the **scratch post** on each level. The game was built using **Unity3D and C#**, leveraging environmental audio and subtle visuals to deliver a cozy and focused experience.

## Goal

Navigate your way through increasingly challenging 3D block puzzles and **strategically plan your moves** to reach the scratch post and progress through the dream realm.

## Feature Highlights

- **Progressive Level Design**: Each stage introduces new logic, layouts, and movement challenges.
- **Relaxing Atmosphere**: Visuals, animations, and ambient sounds are designed to reduce stress and promote calmness.
- **Smooth Controls**:
  - Movement: `WASD`
  - Reload Level: `R`
  - Pause: `ESC`
- **Mouse Support** for in-game menus and UI interaction.

## Technologies Used

- **Unity3D Engine & C#**: Core platform for level design, puzzle mechanics, and scene transitions.
- **Cinemachine & Post-Processing**: For creating dreamy camera movements and visual effects.
- **Audio Integration**: Layered ambient soundscapes to enhance immersion.
- **Game Jam Collaboration**: Worked as part of a creative team on design, scripting, and bug-fixing in a short development cycle.

## Platforms:
  - **Windows & macOS**  
  - Built as a downloadable desktop prototype with native resolution scaling.

Fluffulis received a 5-star player rating and praise for its calm visuals and fluid gameplay. It stands as a strong example of **emotionally-driven game design**, blending logical thinking with sensory relaxation. The game is currently in the prototype phase, with future iterations planned to expand puzzle variety and controller support.
